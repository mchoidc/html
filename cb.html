Instruction

To create a 4x2 checkerboard, have your html be in the following format: 
<div class='board_row'>
   <div class="white"></div>
   <div class="black"></div>
   <div class="white"></div>
   <div class="black"></div>
</div>
<div class='board_row'>
   <div class="black"></div>
   <div class="white"></div>
   <div class="black"></div>
   <div class="white"></div>
</div>

Do not worry about the spacing between the boxes.
